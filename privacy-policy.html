Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

We believe in transparency and your right to privacy. Learn how we handle your data.

🛡️

Data Collection

Design Inspector Pro is built with privacy-first principles. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal user data.

When you use our extension, all analysis and inspection happens entirely within your browser. No data is sent to external servers.

💾

Local Storage

Your preferences and data stay on your device:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Color history from your inspections
  • Extension settings and state

All information is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and never leaves your browser.

📊

Analytics & Tracking

Design Inspector Pro does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Third-party advertising networks
  • User behavior monitoring
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data

Your usage is completely private and anonymous.
